I am an associate professor at the Institute of Applied Mathematics, Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Here are my short and full resumes and please also find me on researchgate.

My research mainly focuses on the existence and stability of traveling wave solutions to nonlinear partial differential equations (PDE) arising from fluid dynamics, detonation and combustion models. In particular, stability of roll waves and hydraulic shock profiles in an inclined channel, stability of detonation waves, stability of free surface water waves (Stokes wave, gravity-capillary waves, and Wilton ripples), etc.
